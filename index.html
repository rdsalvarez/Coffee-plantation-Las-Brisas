About US

Las Brisas (Breezes in English) is the name of a family owned plantation that grows coffee in El Salvador, Central America. It is located on the southwest part of the country a few miles from a small colonial town called Apaneca aproximatly 1 and ½ hour from San Salvador, capital city of El Salvador.
