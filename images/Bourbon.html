BOURBON

Bourbon coffee variety is a result of a natural mutation of Typica coffee , which is one of the antique varieties that exist. Its one of the most relevant varieties, not because its one of the first ones to exist but because its a source of other varieties like Caturra and Pacas.

It arouse in the isle Bourbon, east of what was Madagascar, now calle Reunion Isle. It is grown majorly in Burundi,Ruanda,and Latin America. It has a sweet taste with and fruity flavors.

It can be grown on diferent altitudes, but for optimun results its better to grow at high altitudes from 1,100 to 2,000 mts above sea level. Its fruit is smaller then a Typica and its berries mature earlier, and that is why its recomended to grow them under shade, so the berries mature slowly and acquire more flavors and aromas.

The fruits can be red, yellow or orange color. Yellow Bourbon that originated from Sao Paulo, in Brasil, has a quite a quantity of fructose, which results with a sweet cup. On the other hand, red Bourbons have more glucose which generates a cup with more body.

The attributes of taste of the Bourbon makes it a high quality coffee, but it has some disadvantages of low resistance to pests and diseases like the fungus roya, which requires more care to be grown.

The plantation consists of 6 areas. Five adjacent and one separate. All with diferent varieties.
