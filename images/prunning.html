Prunning

Coffee trees can be as delicate as we humans are.They require special and careful attention to grow adequatly. Having that in mind, tasks that farmers do are many and can be exhausting. One of these is prunning.

Coffee prunning is a tecnique used in agriculture to obtain the following results: mantain a healthy tree, increase productivity, provide strength and vigor so to stimulate grownth and increase plant development and flowering, equal plant grownth, guidance and distribution of branches, increase sunlight access throughout the whole tree, and finally eliminate old branchs which are dried or have no purpose.

When is the best moment to prune the coffee tree? Well it depends on the type of plant, variety, age and the main objective of realizing it. In the majority of cases its made several months after harvest. In EL Salvador its done between the months of Feb and March.

To be able to do a good prunning task one must use the adequate prunning tools. The tools used are special saws and scissors and should be sharp. The cuts must be sharp and clean without tearing apart the plant tissue. The prunning should'nt be too drastic as to weakend the plant.

Prunning also results in a large quantity of prunned branches and wood.The branches are left beside the plants so they decompose and fertilize the soil. The wood is used for firewood for cooking stoves or for fireplaces.
